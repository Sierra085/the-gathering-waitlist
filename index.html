What's this about?

↓

The world has changed. And so have you.

Layoffs. Disillusionment. Neurodivergent crashouts.

It didn't happen immediately. You might've had a gut feeling but you were so embedded in the simulation that you couldn't imagine differently.

"Very slowly, and then all at once."—Adapted from Ernest Hemingway's The Sun Also Rises

Then you woke up one day and suddenly, you couldn't recognise your surroundings. You didn't even know why you were there in the first place.

And no matter how hard you try, you can't seem to reconcile the person you are now with the world that no longer recognises you.

What's happening?

↓

"Third places"—your home away from home—have been disappearing over the last few years, whether you realise it or not. Your favourite hangouts are either gone or endangered. There are crowds of people everywhere, but instead of feeling excited, you're chronically anxious. And lonely.

The disparate post-pandemic culture has left you feeling misunderstood, disconnected, distracted, unfulfilled. So you being you, you try to get to know yourself and others better.

And suddenly, you're learning about ND, ADHD, OCD, NPD ... the acronyms; the labels; and most of all, the consequences of your awareness of your Self and of others, without specialist, human-first & heart-centred guidance, is overwhelming.

We want change. Do you?

↓

"Where is everybody?"

"Why is everything suddenly so hard to do?"

"I want to get help but there are either too many options (I don't have the time and/or money for them all)—or too few (I don't have access to what I really need). Why is help so hard to get?"

Could there be one safe space that solves these common problems without burning a hole in your pocket or causing you more stress?

A place that also feels like home, wherever you are?

Could this be it?

Features

Weekly Guided Body-Doubling Sessions

Weekly guided virtual body-doubling sessions led by trained Community Hosts for companionship, deep focus and accountability. These sessions are themed to encourage balanced living and help you explore parts of yourself society ignores. Whether it's work-related, home care or self-care tasks, there's a session for it. And like-minded friends doing similar tasks together with you.

*One session a month for free account holders

Special Events

From masterclasses and workshops to fireside chats and therapeutic circles, free to paid special events designed to guide you according to pre-planned "seasons".

*Paying members get discounted rates for ticketed events

A Mindfully Curated Calendar

Fixed times for various themed sessions and special events suited to your time zone(s) that offers opt-ins for calendar invites, reminders & event updates.

*All members can view the calendar, updated regularly

The Library

A knowledge base containing free to paid digital resources and references; from evergreen to seasonal.

*Paying members get discounted rates for paid resources
