Primary Features

Weekly Guided Body-Doubling Sessions

Weekly guided virtual body-doubling sessions led by trained Community Hosts for companionship, deep focus and accountability. These sessions are themed to encourage balanced living and help you explore parts of yourself society ignores. Whether it's work-related, home care or self-care tasks, there's a session for it. And like-minded friends doing similar tasks together with you.

*One session a month for free account holders

Special Events

From masterclasses and workshops to fireside chats and therapeutic circles, free to paid special events designed to guide you according to pre-planned "seasons".

*Paying members get discounted rates for ticketed events

A Mindfully Curated Calendar

Fixed times for various themed sessions and special events suited to your time zone(s) that offers opt-ins for calendar invites, reminders & event updates.

*All members can view the calendar, updated regularly

The Library

A knowledge base containing free to paid digital resources and references; from evergreen to seasonal.

*Paying members get discounted rates for paid resources
